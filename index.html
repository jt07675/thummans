The Thumann's Difference

Thumann's has been manufacturing the highest quality, most delicious delicatessen products for more than a half a century. We use only the finest & freshest ingredients, guaranteeing our customers the best tasting, healthiest products available. Our family recipes use the least amounts of salt & sugar, and we never use any artificial flavors or colors, by-products, fillers or gluten. Our products are Trans fat free and contain absolutely no MSG.
